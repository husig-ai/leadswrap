01 Data Safety Summary

✓
Data is collected and shared
Leadswrap collects user data to operate the platform. See Section 2 for the full breakdown of what is collected and why.
🔒
All data is encrypted in transit
Every connection between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest in our database is protected with access controls and role-based permissions.
👤
Account creation: Username and password
Leadswrap accounts are created with an email address and password. Passwords are stored as secure hashes — we never store plaintext credentials. The app does not currently support OAuth or third-party sign-in.
🗑️
You can request deletion of your account and data
Users may request full account deletion or removal of specific data at any time. See Sections 4 and 5 for instructions.

03 Security Practices

🔐
Encrypted in transit — Yes
All data exchanged between the Leadswrap app and our servers travels over HTTPS/TLS. This applies to all API calls, authentication, and file uploads.
🛡️
Passwords hashed, never stored in plaintext
Passwords are one-way hashed before storage. Even our engineers cannot read your password.
🔑
Token-based authentication with short expiry
Session tokens expire automatically. Suspicious sessions can be invalidated remotely.
🗄️
Role-based database access controls
Database access is restricted to authenticated services only. No direct external database exposure.

04 Delete Your Account

You may request deletion of your Leadswrap account at any time. When your account is deleted, the following happens:

What gets deleted
Upon confirmed account deletion, we permanently remove:
  • Your agent profile (name, email, phone number, hashed password)
  • All lead records associated with your account, including contact details, LPMAMA scores, and audit history
  • All messages, notes, and reminders linked to your leads
  • Property listings and matched lead associations you created
  • Your FCM push notification token
How to request account deletion
Follow these steps to submit your deletion request. We will confirm and process it within 30 days.
  1. Send an email to contact@husig.ai with the subject line: "Leadswrap — Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include the email address associated with your Leadswrap account so we can verify your identity.
  3. We will send a confirmation email within 2 business days. Reply to confirm you authorise the deletion.
  4. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your confirmation.

05 Delete Specific Data (Without Deleting Account)

You do not need to delete your entire account to remove specific data. Leadswrap supports data deletion at the record level:

✓
Delete individual lead records
You can delete any lead record directly within the app at any time. Deletion removes the lead, its contact details, messages, notes, and scoring history permanently.
✓
Delete messages and notes
Individual notes and messages can be removed from within the app without affecting other lead data.
✓
Remove push notification access
You can disable push notifications at any time from your device settings or within the app. This will remove your FCM token from our servers.
Request deletion of specific data by email
If you need us to delete specific data on your behalf — for example, data you cannot access directly in the app — contact us with the details below.
  1. Email contact@husig.ai with subject: "Leadswrap — Data Deletion Request"
  2. Specify which data you want deleted (e.g., a specific lead record, all messages, your usage logs).
  3. We will confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days.

06 Data Retained After Deletion

Some data may be retained for a limited period after a deletion request for legal compliance and service integrity. The table below is transparent about what is kept, and for how long.

Account data
90 days
Retained after account deletion to allow recovery if the request was made in error. Permanently deleted after this window.
Audit logs
Up to 12 months
Compliance and dispute resolution records. These do not contain lead personal data — only action metadata (e.g., "status changed to QUALIFIED").
Server / usage logs
30–90 days
Automatically purged on a rolling basis regardless of account status. These contain IP addresses and request metadata, not content.

After the retention periods above, all data is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems and backups. We do not archive deleted user data beyond these windows.
