AI Scoring Engine

Built on the proven
LPMAMA framework

Every lead is evaluated across six critical dimensions. The result is a live hotness score that tells agents exactly where to focus their energy.


Scores update automatically as lead data is enriched, so your pipeline is always reflecting current reality — not stale guesswork.

L
Location
Preferred area, property type, bedroom requirements, and location flexibility all contribute to score.
P
Price
Budget range, certainty level, and affordability signals tell us how close a lead is to transacting.
M
Motivation
Is this a first home buyer, investor, or just browsing? Urgency and motivation type shape the score significantly.
A
Agent
Whether the lead is already working with another agent affects their exclusivity and convertibility.
M
Mortgage
Cash buyer, pre-approved, or not started — financing status is one of the strongest conversion indicators.
A
Appointment
Has an appointment been requested or scheduled? Commitment signals translate directly into score.
